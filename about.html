Hi!


I'm Scott Fralin and I'm the Exhibit Curator and Learning Environments Librarian at the Virginia Tech Libraries. I have a B.A. in Classics from Virginia Tech and an M.S. in Infomation Science from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

The projects you see here have been created as part of my job at Virginia Tech. Most of them are part of the Course Exhibit Initiative, which is a series of installations in Newman Library that feature the work of classes offered at Virginia Tech.
